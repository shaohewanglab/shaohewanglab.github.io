Zhiyuan is pursuing his undergraduate degrees in Biological Engineering and Biochemistry at Purdue University. His research experience includes differentiating and characterizing stem cell-derived cardiomyocytes and investigating disease phenotypes associated with chromosome 8p mutations. In a separate project, he developed and characterized tunable, degradable hydrogels as a platform for studying wound healing and tissue regeneration. Through this work, he became especially interested in nascent extracellular matrix protein deposition and its role in tissue repair. As a Cech Fellows Program participant, Zhiyuan is excited to gain research experience in the Wang Lab and explore projects centered on the extracellular matrix and its roles in biological systems.

Outside of work, Zhiyuan enjoys reading, cooking, and working on personal projects involving 3D printing and PCB design.
